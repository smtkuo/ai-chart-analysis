About ChartMaster AI

ChartMaster AI is an advanced chart analysis tool that helps you extract insights from your financial charts and technical analysis visualizations.

How to use:

  1. Enter your RapidAPI key in the designated field
  2. Upload your chart image or provide an image URL
  3. Select your preferred language for analysis
  4. Click "Analyze Chart" to get detailed insights

The analysis will provide you with detailed information about trends, patterns, support/resistance levels, and potential trading opportunities identified in your chart.
